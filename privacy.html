Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Overview

Storm Raiders is a casual web game site. We do not require account signup and we minimize data collection.

Information We Handle

  • Local data: high scores and simple preferences stored in your browser’s localStorage, never sent to our servers.
  • Contact messages: if you choose to contact us, we receive the information you submit.

Cookies

We do not set cookies by default. Third-party tools you add later may use cookies under their own policies.

Your Choices

  • Clear site data to remove local scores.
  • Use private browsing to avoid storing local data.

Contact

Questions? Use the contact form on the Home page.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Our Philosophy on Data

Your privacy is important to us. Storm Raiders is designed to be a fun, straightforward experience, which is why we don't require you to sign up for an account. Our policy is simple: we collect as little information as possible, so you can focus on playing the game.

What Information We Handle (and Don't)

  • Game Data Stays With You: Your high scores and game preferences are saved using your browser's "local storage." This data lives exclusively on your computer or device and is never transmitted to our servers. We can't see it, and we don't want to.
  • Contact Form Messages: If you decide to send us a message through our contact form, we will naturally receive the information you provide (like your name and email) so we can get back to you. We only use this to respond to your query.

A Note on Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising. The core Storm Raiders experience is cookie-free. If you or we decide to integrate third-party tools in the future (like an embedded video player), those services might use cookies, which would be governed by their own privacy policies.

You're in Control

  • Clearing Your Scores: You can easily erase all your locally stored game data at any time by clearing your browser's cache and site data for our website.
  • Playing Anonymously: If you prefer not to store any data at all, you can use your browser's private or incognito mode.

Have Questions?

If you have any questions or concerns about our approach to privacy, please don't hesitate to reach out using the contact form on our Home page. We're happy to clarify anything.
