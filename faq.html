FAQ

Answers to common questions about Storm Raiders and Sky Wars.

Does this work on my phone?

Absolutely! The game is designed to be fully responsive. The board scales to fit your screen, and you can simply tap the dice to roll. It’s a smooth experience whether you're on a desktop or a phone.

How are game results saved?

Your progress, including wins, total moves, and leaderboard entries, is saved directly in your browser's local storage. This means your data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Can I reset the leaderboard?

You sure can. If you want a fresh start, you can open your browser's developer console and run the following command: localStorage.removeItem('sl_leaderboard'). This will clear all your previous scores.

What are the game controls?

It's simple! Click or tap the dice to make your move. For keyboard players, you can also press R to restart a match and P to pause or resume the game at any time.

Can I customize the game's appearance?

For those who love to tinker, yes! The game is built with customization in mind. You can dive into the assets/css/style.css file to adjust the CSS variables for colors and fonts, or even change the text in the HTML files to create your own unique theme.
